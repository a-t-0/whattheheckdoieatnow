Blackening Spice Blend

January 5, 2016 Tara Leave a Comment

This spice blend is great for adding flavour to anything and everything especially sprinkled on tofu steaks prior to sautéing (slice and add the blackened tofu to your Caeser Salad!) or in this  Spicy Black Pepper Tofu.

Mix all the ingredients in a small bowl and store in an airtight container. Use as needed:)

Adapted from Martin, Roberto. ‘Blackening Spice’. Vegan Cooking for Carnivores.

Blackening Spice Blend
 
Print
Prep time
5 mins
Total time
5 mins
 
Use this blackening spice mix for delicious, sautéed blackened tofu
Website: WhatTheHeckDoIEatNow.com
Adapted From: Martin, Roberto. "Blackening Spice". Vegan Cooking for Carnivores.
Serves: ¼ cup
Ingredients
  • ½ tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • ½ tbsp dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • ½ tbsp salt
  • ½ tbsp freshly ground black pepper
Instructions
  1. Mix all the ingredients in a small bowl and store in an airtight container.
