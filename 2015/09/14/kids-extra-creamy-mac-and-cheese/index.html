KIDS Extra Creamy Mac and Cheese

September 14, 2015 Tara 4 Comments

My kids absolutely love this healthy version of Mac and Cheese. If it’s up to them, this is what we have for supper, and it’s my son’s most requested item for in his thermos at school. It makes a lot of sauce but trust me, pour it in and mix well. Add a bit of ketchup for that familiar comfort food flavour. This reheats well so I’ll often make a batch Sunday night and the kiddos get to take it to school all week! Let me know what your kids think!

KIDS Extra Creamy Mac and Cheese
 
Print
Prep time
5 mins
Cook time
20 mins
Total time
25 mins
 
A healthy version of the mac and cheese from your youth.
Website: WhatTheHeckDoIEatNow.com
Adapted From: One Green Planet. "Extra Creamy Vegan Mac and Cheese".
Serves: 6 child size servings.
Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups potato, peeled and cut into bite size pieces
  • 1 cup carrot, cut into ½ inch rounds
  • ½ cup yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cups water
  • ½ cup Vegan Becel margarine
  • ½ cup nutritional yeast
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1¼ tsp salt
  • ¾-1 tsp garlic powder
  • ¾-1 tsp onion powder
  • 3 tbsp coconut milk (from a can)
  • 3 turns black pepper
  • 4 cups dried white macaroni or gluten free rice macaroni
  • ketchup
Instructions
  1. Place potato, carrot and onion in a pot with 2 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  2. Cover, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Turn off heat and add vegan Becel, nutritional yeast, turmeric, salt, garlic powder, onion powder and pepper.
  4. Transfer to Vitamix and blend until smooth.
  5. Add 3 tbsp of canned coconut milk and blend again.
  6. Cook macaroni as per package directions. Drain and return to pot.
  7. Pour sauce over top and mix well.
  8. Serve with ketchup.
